Don't Drink and Drive


Drinking and driving is a huge issue today. Many deaths are caused by negligent drivers under the influence of alcohol or another substance. In 2015, more than 10,000 people died as a result of impaired driving, and more than a million reports of driving under the influence.

One third of drunk drivers are repeat offenders as well, and some have suspended licenses as well. Illegal driving massively increases risk of crashes.
